My Journey

Primary School

SK Bukit Rimau

My journey in seeking knowledge began at Sekolah Kebangsaan Bukit Rimau. It is located in Bukit Rimau, Shah Alam. It is the school closest to my house and SK Bukit Rimau is the school that introduced me to information. Therefore, SK Bukit Rimau will always hold a special place in my heart because it has provided me with many ups and downs that have taught me the value of independence in my life.

Secondary School

SMK Kota Kemuning

Moving on, I get into SMK Kota Kemuning and spent my high school years at SMK Kota Kemuning. Studying in this place is one of the decisions I have ever made. All students and teachers are the most fun memories that I will never forget. I used to be a quiet student in my primary school, but my high school helped me to get out of my comfort zones and start socialising with other people around me. As a result, I meet my best friends who bring joy and laughter into my life. I also frequently participate in school-organized activities and took my education very serious due to the counseling sessions organized by my school every year. The session has assisted me in determing and identify my areas of passion and interest. Therefore, I am proud to state that I am an alumnus of SMK Kota Kemuning as the school has provided the best for its students.

University

Universiti Teknologi Mara, Rembau

Long short story, i got accepted into UiTM in July 2019 and am currently enrolled in Diploma in Information Management in UiTM Rembau. Unfortunately, I was only able to study physically for one semester and a half before the outbreak of the Covid-19. This has led to physical classes being converted into online classes. Of course, just like everyone else, online class is not an easy thing for me as well. Despite these shortcomings, studying in UiTM is also another memorable memory for me as I met a lot of friends from other states and got to learn and understand their language and culture.
